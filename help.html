Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to shop?

No, you can browse products without signing in. However, to make a purchase or save your cart, you will need to create a free account.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular wallets like Paytm & Google Pay.

Can I update my account details later?

Yes, you can update your email, password, and personal details anytime by signing in and going to your account settings.
