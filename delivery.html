Delivery Information

Know about shipping & delivery policies

Shipping Options

We offer standard delivery across India. Orders are shipped within 24-48 hours.

Estimated Delivery Time

3-5 business days for metro cities, 5-7 business days for other locations.

Delivery Charges

Free delivery on all orders above ₹999. A shipping fee of ₹50 is applied for orders below ₹999.

International Delivery

Currently, we only deliver within India.
